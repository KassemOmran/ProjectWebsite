Welcome to the Faculty of Sciences

Founded in 1959, the Faculty of Science was one of the first and largest faculties of the Lebanese University, and in the 1970s it formed the first nucleus of the Rafik Hariri University Campus - Hadath


The Faculty of Science is committed to continuously updated scientific programs and always strives to develop the knowledge of its students by including more specializations in its bachelor and master programs and in the scientific research laboratory.
