Welcome to the Faculty of Sciences

Founded in 1959, the Faculty of Science was one of the first and largest faculties of the Lebanese University, and in the 1970s it formed the first nucleus of the Rafik Hariri University Campus - Hadath


The Faculty of Science is committed to continuously updated scientific programs and always strives to develop the knowledge of its students by including more specializations in its bachelor and master programs and in the scientific research laboratory.


The faculty grants the following degrees:

Mathematics

Core Subjects:

  • Calculus
  • Linear Algebra
  • Statistics
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Abstract Algebra

Career Paths: Data Analyst, Actuary, Statistician, Operations Research Analyst.

Computer Science

Core Subjects:

  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Management
  • Web Development
  • Operating Systems

Career Paths: Software Engineer, Data Analyst, Systems Architect, IT Consultant.

Statistics

Core Subjects:

  • Probability Theory
  • Statistical Inference
  • Regression Analysis
  • Design of Experiments
  • Time Series Analysis

Career Paths: Data Scientist, Statistician, Market Research Analyst, Biostatistician.

Physics
